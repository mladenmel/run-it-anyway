About The Brand

About Me

I am a student creator with a real interest in cars and the content built around them.

I spend a lot of time around cars, whether that means shooting them, reviewing them, or working through ideas for future builds. What I enjoy most is finding a clean way to show the details that make a car worth paying attention to.

The content side comes naturally from that. I like visuals that feel sharp, reviews that stay honest, and build coverage that shows the process instead of pretending everything is already finished.

Brand Purpose

Why "Run It Anyway" exists.

Run It Anyway is a personal automotive brand built around honest content, interesting cars, and projects that are still evolving. Not every car needs to be perfect to be worth talking about, and some of the best stories come from the ones that are still a work in progress.

This site brings everything together in one place: dealership features, car reviews, and build updates. It is meant to feel organized and clean, while still sounding like it comes from someone who is genuinely into the cars behind the content.
